Bro's Recipe for the best cinnaamon rolls

Ingredients

for the dough:

  • 3/4 cup warm milk
  • 2 14 teaspoons yeast
  • 14 cup of granulated sugar
  • 1 egg plus 1 egg yolk
  • 1/4 cup butter
  • 3 cups bread floor

for the filling:

  • 23 cup drak brown sugar
  • 1/4 cup butter

Intructions

  1. Melt the milk with the yeast, sugar, eggs.
  2. Melt the butter and add to the mixture.
  3. dd in the flour and mix until combined into a dough
  4. knead the dough for 10 minutes
